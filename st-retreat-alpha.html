Why a retreat

The Speck&Tech Retreat 2k18 is THE event you can't miss! On October 12-14, our Speckers will reunite to strengthen their relationships, learn from each other, have fun, discuss together trending hot topics, and spend an unforgettable weekend with old and new friends.

The programme covers a multitude of distinct areas, from technical and stimulating talks to enriching workshops, from exciting outdoor activities in the Alps to long relaxing hours in SPA and sauna, from gamified visits of top tech companies to tasty gourmet dinners.

Val di sole

The Alpholiday Hotel, located between the Adamello and Stelvio parks, offers panoramic views of the Brenta Dolomites. It features 3 pools, a wellness centre with sauna, Turkish bath, jacuzzi, gym and beauty treatments. The restaurant serves both Trentino and classic Italian gourmet cuisine.

FAQ

- Who can attend the event and what are the selection criteria?

Everyone is welcome! For legal and liability reasons, you must be at least 18 years old. As there are limited spots, we will ask you to submit a brief motivation letter and we reserve the right to approve each participant. We recommend that you register as soon as possible: approved participants will be granted a spot based on the order of requests.

- Is there a discount for students?

To encourage younger Speckers to participate, we are offering a limited number of student tickets at a reduced price of 100‚Ç¨. You have to be currently enrolled to a university to qualify for the discounted ticket (proof will be required).

- What is required in order to participate?

You are requested to complete the registration form of the Eventbrite, and pay a deposit corresponding to the price of the selected ticket.
