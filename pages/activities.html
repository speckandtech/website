Activities

Speck&Tech is more than just monthly meetups. Over the years we have developed several community-driven activities that extend our mission beyond the stage. Whether it's helping elderly people approach the digital world or combining hiking with deep tech conversations, these initiatives bring technology closer to people in new and meaningful ways.

Pan&Botóni

A digital literacy course designed for elderly people who want to learn how to use computers, smartphones, and digital tools. Through practical lessons, participants gain the confidence to navigate the digital world independently.

Learn more →

Speck&Trek

A community hike that brings together nature lovers and tech enthusiasts. Participants reach the top of a local mountain and then enjoy an informal tech talk with a speaker, surrounded by breathtaking views.
