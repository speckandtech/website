Speck&Trek

Speck&Trek

Explore the territory, while sharing the passion for technology



A different way to network, outdoors

Speck&Trek is an initiative born from the idea of exploring the territory around us together with our community. We meet up, hike towards a destination—whether it's a peak, a mountain hut, or a scenic viewpoint—and once we arrive, we talk about tech, eat together, and hang out. A different way to network, outdoors.

Cima Calisio

Date: July 18, 2021

Difficulty: ⭐⭐

Elevation gain: +650m

Distance: 8 km

Duration: 3h

Our first Speck&Trek adventure took us to Cima Calisio, where we discussed the future of AI and machine learning while enjoying panoramic views of Trento valley. The perfect combination of nature and technology, with participants sharing insights about emerging trends in tech over a well-deserved mountain lunch.
