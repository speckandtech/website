Why a retreat?

The Speck&Tech Retreat is back for its 6th edition, and once again it‚Äôs shaping up to be an unmissable experience. On May 22-24, our Speckers will gather to reconnect, strengthen bonds, learn from one another, spark meaningful conversations around today‚Äôs hottest topics, and enjoy an unforgettable weekend with old friends and new faces.

It all started in 2018, when more than 50 participants met in Dimaro for a three-day adventure. One year later, in 2019, the community grew to over 60 people. After the forced pause of the pandemic years, we brought the Retreat spirit back in 2022 with 80 attendees, and in 2023, we finally hit the magic number: 100 beautiful Speckers! Last edition, in 2024, we were still 100 amazing Speckers, sharing the same energy, curiosity, and passion.

In May 2026, we return once again with the same ambition: we want to offer you a broad range of activities that will not only boost your career from a professional and personal point of view, but will also allow you to take a short holiday with the people you love, surrounded by a stunning environment and relaxing views.

The programme covers a multitude of distinct areas, from technical and stimulating talks to enriching workshops, from outdoor activities in the Alps to long relaxing hours in SPA and sauna, from gamified visits of top tech companies to tasty gourmet dinners.

FAQs

Who can attend the event and what are the selection criteria?

Everyone is welcome! However, you should be at least 18 years old, or accompanied by an adult, for legal and liability reasons. As there are limited spots available, we strongly recommend that you register as soon as possible: participants will be granted a spot on a first-come, first-served basis. The Organizing team reserves the right to approve each participant.

What is required in order to participate?

You are requested to complete the registration form at the TicketTailor page, and issue a bank transfer to the AlpHoliday Hotel in Dimaro, which covers your participation fee. You will receive all information at your email address once you complete the registration process, so please check your spam folder, too!

Why do I have to issue a bank transfer now?

We ask you to pay your participation fee in order to confirm you are attending the event, so we can fully rely on your registration, plan accordingly and provide you with the best event experience.

Will I need to pay anything else for the event?

The participation fee covers all costs included in "The participation fee includes" section above. Once attending the event, you will surely need to pay the Visitor's tax and, eventually, other costs in "The participation fee does not include" section.

What happens if I cannot attend the event after registering?

You can cancel your registration until 40 days prior to the event start (i.e. April 12), and you will get your money back. Any cancellation after this deadline for reasons of force majeure will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

What if I am not one of the first 60 successful applicants?

Given the limited spots available, we cannot guarantee the participation of each member. In case all tickets are sold out, a waiting list will be set up. If a ticket becomes available, you will be contacted automatically with further instructions on how to get it.

Can I choose with whom to stay in my room?

Please, do mention whom you would like to share your room with directly on the registration form at the TicketTailor page. We will do our best to fulfill all requests.

When do applications close?

Applications officially close on March 8, 23:59 CET.

Which outdoor activity may I attend?

We are planning some great outdoor activities for you:

  • RAFTING POWER
  • CANYONING
  • PARCO AVVENTURA
  • PAINTBALL

We have struck a 20% OFF the price! Let us know on the TicketTailor registration form which ones you would be most interested in.

Do I have to attend one?

Of course not, you may also simply not attend any and just spend the day chilling outside or at the SPA.

What should I bring?

Regardless of the outdoor activity you will choose for Saturday, we suggest you bring a swimsuit, mountain shoes, and comfortable and warm clothes. All events are casual. More information will follow closer to the event's date.

Is there a discount for students?

To encourage younger Speckers to participate, we offer a limited number of student tickets at a reduced price of 150‚Ç¨. You have to be currently enrolled in a university to qualify for the discounted ticket (proof will be required).

May I attend only specific sessions or days of the event?

Not really. The event comes as a whole. Partial attendance for reasons of force majeure will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Am I required to take holidays from my work to attend this event?

Not really, the event starts at 5 PM on Friday, when we will meet at the train station in Trento. However, we encourage you to tell your employer about this event, so they might even grant you a half-day permit because of being a company's ambassador, or record your attendance at Sunday‚Äôs conference as a learning experience.

Any more questions you might have?

Please email us at retreat@speckand.tech.
